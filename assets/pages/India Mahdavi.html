Visionary Interior Designer

India Mahdavi

India Mahdavi is a renowned Iranian-born interior designer based in Paris, celebrated for her vibrant, playful, and eclectic approach to design. Known for her signature use of color and texture, Mahdavi creates inviting spaces that blend modern aesthetics with a touch of whimsy. Her work spans various sectors, including residential interiors, hospitality, and commercial projects, with a distinctive style that often incorporates bold patterns and unique furnishings.

Mahdavi’s portfolio includes notable projects like the iconic Hôtel Montaigne in Paris and the popular Ladurée tearooms, where she skillfully combines contemporary design with cultural references. She emphasizes the importance of creating atmospheres that evoke emotion and invite interaction, making her spaces not just visually appealing but also deeply experiential. With a strong presence in the design community, Mahdavi continues to influence and inspire through her innovative designs and artistic vision.

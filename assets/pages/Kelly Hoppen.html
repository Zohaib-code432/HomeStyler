Renowned Interior Designer

Kelly Hoppen

Kelly Hoppen is a highly acclaimed British interior designer, known for her elegant, contemporary designs that effortlessly blend East and West aesthetics. With over 40 years of experience, she has built a diverse portfolio that includes residential homes, commercial spaces, and luxury yachts.

Her design philosophy emphasizes the importance of functionality while maintaining a sense of luxury and comfort. Hoppen has also authored several design books and appeared as a judge on television shows like "BBC's Dragons' Den," further establishing her influence in the design world.

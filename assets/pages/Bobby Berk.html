Innovative Interior Designer

Bobby Berk

Bobby Berk is a prominent American interior designer and television personality, best known as the design expert on Netflix's "Queer Eye." Based in Los Angeles, he specializes in creating stylish and functional spaces that reflect his clients' personalities. Berk's modern aesthetic combines contemporary design with practical solutions, often emphasizing personalization and sustainability.

In addition to his television work, Berk has launched his own line of home décor products and authored a book, further establishing his influence in the design world. Through his innovative designs, he inspires others to see the potential in their spaces and enhance their daily lives.

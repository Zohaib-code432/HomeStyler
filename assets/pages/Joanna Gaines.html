Renowned Interior Designer and Entrepreneur

Joanna Gaines

Joanna Gaines is a renowned American interior designer, author, and television personality, best known for co-hosting "Fixer Upper" with her husband, Chip Gaines. Based in Waco, Texas, she is celebrated for her modern farmhouse style, which combines rustic charm with contemporary elegance.

Gaines focuses on creating functional, family-friendly spaces that reflect her clients' lifestyles, often featuring a neutral color palette and vintage accents. In addition to her design work, she has authored bestselling books and co-founded the Magnolia brand, which encompasses a lifestyle magazine and a popular market. With her unique vision and entrepreneurial spirit, Joanna Gaines continues to inspire homeowners and designers alike.

Renowned Interior Designer and Potter

Jonathan Adler

Jonathan Adler is a prominent American interior designer, potter, and author, celebrated for his bold and whimsical aesthetic. Known for his use of vibrant colors, playful patterns, and unique textures, Adler creates spaces that exude a sense of joy and personality. His design philosophy emphasizes the importance of creativity and self-expression, encouraging clients to embrace their individuality.

Based in New York City, Adler has built a successful brand that includes a wide range of home décor products, from furniture and textiles to ceramics. His signature style often combines mid-century modern influences with a contemporary twist, making his work instantly recognizable.
